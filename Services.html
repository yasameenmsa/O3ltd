Our Comprehensive Security Services

At O3 Guarding Ltd, we are committed to providing businesses of all sizes with a range of comprehensive security solutions tailored to meet their unique needs. Our experienced team of security professionals is dedicated to ensuring the safety and protection of your assets, data, and people. Explore the diverse services we offer to safeguard your business:

Manned Guarding

Our highly trained security officers offer 24/7 on-site presence, patrolling, access control, and incident response to ensure the safety of your premises.

Retail Security

Protect your retail establishment against theft and vandalism with our retail security officers who provide surveillance, customer assistance, and loss prevention.

Dog Handling

Our trained dog handling teams provide an additional layer of security, offering both a visual deterrent and enhanced threat detection capabilities.

Close Protection

For high-profile individuals or executives, our close protection officers provide personal security, risk assessment, and safe transportation.

Mobile Response

Our mobile response units are equipped to quickly respond to alarms and incidents, providing immediate support and ensuring the security of your property.

Key Holding

Entrust us with the responsibility of holding your keys securely and responding to emergencies, reducing risks and ensuring a swift response.

Stewards

Our stewards offer crowd management and event security services, ensuring the safety and smooth flow of events, exhibitions, and gatherings.
